    Dr.M. Johnson
    Programme Coordinator

    Looking into the potential on value addition to millets, during 2014 training programmes on Millet based products were organized in Home Science discipline by KVK Reddipalli. The endeavour of these training programmes were to train farm women in preparing products based on millets and to get them aware about nutritional benefits of these millet based products and also enhancing their skills in preparing different recipes and make it as a secondary income source so that their house hold nutritional security and financial security will be enhanced. Smt B. Nirmala Naik is one such enthusiastic women entrepreneur of 2014 training batchs, trained from KVK, Reddipalli. Initial stages Smt.B. Nirmala naik use to buy the millet products(cookies, laddu & Semolina) from KVK and with minimum profit she use to sale the products at her outlet at Rythu bazaar, after six months she started a small scale biscuit making unit with 10-15 kgs production per day, by gaining experience and technical guidance from Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Reddipalli (ANGRAU) extended the production to 90-100 kgs per day in preparation of millet products i.e., millet cookies with jaggery, multi millet laddu and other traditional snack items by maintaining quality and standards and following FSSAI and she also explored her knowledge after attending Millet fest Melas. she improved with the support of her husband Sri B. Narsimha Naik her business by forming JLG group extended to large scale and selling their products to many MNC companies and other agencies in AP, Telangana, Karnataka, etc and they are doing their business in making value added products from millets for the past seven years. Hearty congratulations to Tirumala Natural Food Products Joint Liability group for launch of website and extending their business through online. On behalf of KVK we wish them success for their future achievements.
