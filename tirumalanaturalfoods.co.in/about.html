About Us

Welcome To Tirumala Natural Food Products

Little Story

We are a Team of Tirumala Natural Food Products we provide Natural foods that are free from added artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives and These are minimally processed offer good nutrition and these free from Sugar

Organic foods, which are the most natural foods as they are grown without the use of chemicals, pesticides and fertilizers , and are produced in a sustainable manner



Preamble

Anantapur District is one of the drought – prone districts in India, where the major profession of the people is farming or farm-based employment. The major corps produced in the district are groundnut, paddy, millets and different greens and vegetables. Dryland Horticulture has been widely promoted by the Department of Horticulture keeping in view the driest conditions in the district.


Profile

Tirumala Natural Food Products Joint Liability Group (TNFP JLG) is started on 22nd January, 2014. In the presence of the District Collector Shri. Lakshmikantham, IAS garu the inauguration was attended by Mr. Sri Ramamurthy, JDA, Mr. Lakshmi Reddy, Program Co-Ordinator, KVK Reddypalli and Mr. Malla Reddy, Program Director, AF Ecology Center. The Program was Organized in the Rythu Bazar, Anantarpur. Tirumala Natural Food Products JLG started a small scale millet value-added food processing unit at 5-12-13, Anjaneya Swamy Nagar, Bellary By-pass road, Anantapur on the date of 07th November, 2017


AIM

Thirumla Natural Food Products JLG procure locally produced agriculture products. Preparation and promotion of millet value-added products. To help them in getting better price for their produce. There by enhancing the economic status and health of the locally farmers, and give the healthy millet food products to the customers.


Vision

  • To improve the income of the farmers and farmer-based workers to improvise the socio-economic conditions  
  • To create scope of self-employment by engaging un-employed youth/ local Wenders in the marketing the value-added produce  
  • To Create marketing linkages value added produce  
  • To supply the healthy millet food products to the children, women and anemia patients  
  • To supply the high-quality millet value added products to super markets, Malls, Agencies and local Wenders


Plan Implement Support

By keeping this in view KVK, Reddypalli conducted various extension activities to promote value added food products with millets. With the technical support by KVK, Reddypalli. Smt. B. Nirmala Naik who had trained at KVK, Reddypalli was formed a Joint Liability Group named as Tirumala Natural Food Products, she as the President of JLG, leading the organization for empowering the women through value addition of millets as livelihood option. The JLG produces millet products under the brand name of “Millet Magic”.


Packing Structure

First Biscuits are packed with Food Grade Silver cover and this cover shield with a container made by Areca nut leaf finally moisture free wrapper will be covered. Above said all materials are eco-friendly and fully natural.


Literature

Literature developed in telugu “Chirudhaanyala to sampradayavantalu” and distributed during Kisanmelas and training programmes


Output

The Tirumala Natural Food Products JLG supplies Ragi, Bajra, Jowar, Foxtail, Multi Millet Biscuits and Multi Millet Laddu, Jowar Masala Flakes.The JLG has tie up with various organization to supply of above said products i.e., KGBV school children, District Collector office meetings, NABARD Programme, Bank meetings, National level Celebrations, Malls, ACC cement and Sakhi Trust trainings and various prime stores, Agencies in the stats of AP, Telangana and Karnataka. Recently our JLG was tie up with HT Agri Business Services, Hyderabad. Smt. B. Nirmala Naik Received several awards for her contributions in millet value addition including “Mahila Rythu Puraskar” award presented by Andhra Pradesh State Government in the occasion of National Mahila Rythu Diwas - 2018.
